About the Software Product Line Conference

SPLC, The Software Product Line Conference, is the premier forum for practitioners, researchers and educators to present and discuss the most recent ideas, innovations, trends, experiences, and concerns in the area of software product lines, software product family engineering and, more in general, systems family engineering.
Real-world problems
Mobile devices, such as Android, have thousands of product variations that need to be managed.
Collaborative
SPLC looks back on a history of a successful exchange of ideas and cooperation among product line practitioners and researchers on product line techniques, methods, and tools. This has led to innovative research ideas and successful industrial product lines.

Hosted in the Music City
