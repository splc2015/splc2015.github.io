Opportunities for Sponsoring SPLC 2015

Platinum Support ($5,000):

  • Sponsor will be mentioned in opening and closing session of the main conference
  • Sponsor logo included in the conference program
  • Sponsor logo on the conference website
  • One-page advertisement provided by the Sponsor along with the Sponsor logo in the conference program
  • Sponsor logo in conference proceedings
  • Up to two letter-sized pages provided by the Sponsor distributed to attendees
  • Three free conference tickets for admission to the main SPLC 2015 conference
  • Free booth space with sponsor provided banner during main conference (2mx2,5m), if desired

Gold Support ($3,500):

  • Sponsor logo included in the conference program
  • Sponsor logo on the conference website
  • Half-page advertisement provided by the Sponsor along with the Sponsor logo in the conference program
  • Sponsor logo in conference proceedings
  • One letter-sized pages provided by the Sponsor in the conference bag
  • Two free conference tickets for admission to the main SPLC 2015 conference
  • Two free conference tickets for admission to the main SPLC 2015 conference

Silver Support ($2,000):

  • Sponsor logo included in the conference program
  • Sponsor logo on the conference website
  • Sponsor logo in conference proceedings
  • One letter-sized page provided by the Sponsor in the conference bag
  • Sponsor logo on the general conference banner

Bronze Support ($1,000):

  • Sponsor logo included in the conference program
  • Sponsor logo on the conference website
  • Sponsor logo in conference proceedings
  • Sponsor logo on the general conference banner

Further options / Information:

We also welcome alternative proposals for supporting the conference. Please contact Douglas C. Schmidt (schmidt@dre.vanderbilt.edu) for further information.
